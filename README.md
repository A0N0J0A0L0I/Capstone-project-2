Objective

To analyze the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment and trader behavior on Hyperliquid.

Methodology

Cleaned and merged datasets on daily level.

Created trader-level and daily metrics:

PnL

Win rate

Leverage

Trade frequency

Long/Short ratio

Segmented traders by leverage, activity, and consistency.

Compared behavior across Fear vs Greed regimes.

Key Insights

High leverage traders underperform significantly during Fear.

Trade frequency increases during Greed.

Long bias dominates during Greed; short bias rises during Fear.

Strategy Recommendations

Reduce leverage during Fear periods.

Use sentiment as regime filter before executing trades.

Avoid high-frequency trading during volatile Fear phases.
